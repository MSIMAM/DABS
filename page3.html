Page Three

yasa mai suna daha

an kirashi da auwaha

dogaro nai allah

kunga zancen nan sahha

yayi daidai ba illah

arrasulu ma'aikinmu

arrasulu masoyinmu

arrasulu mashaidinmu

arrasulu macecinmu

gobe gun zatin allah

gaisuwata na miqa

gunka jikan ishaqa

zuciya naimin saqa

inyi baitotin waqa

don yabon manzon allah

gaisuwar ta tsantsanta

godiyar ta wajibta

zantukan sun inganta

shugaba ka fifita

duk akan bayin allah

ni cikin wannan sabga

mai yawan kyawun siga/p>

nayi zance ba luggah

so nakeyi in waiga

gun mazowwa bin allah

masu baiwa ba illah

masu haske ya fitila

masu gyara mu'amillah

masu girma gun allah

sai tijani abil faydha

dan muhammadu mai shiriya

shi yasamu akan hanya

wacce ba'a jayayya

sai biya sai soyayya

sai zumunci don allah

shaikhu abil abbasi

masu yaye ilbasi

masu bautar quddusi

shugabana nibrasi

yan gidan manzon allah
