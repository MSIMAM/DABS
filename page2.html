Page Two

nayi roqo ya rabba

tunda nace na tuba

bani khairi da ijabah

don abubakari garba

sahibin manzon allah

ya ta'ala kallaqu

gani nazama mallaqu

na zamanto mushtaqu

gun sahabi faruqu

umarun manzon allah

shugabana usmanu

sayyidi dan affanu

mai karatun qur'anu

mai yawan hasken dinu

mai yawan sujjadah sallah

rabbana kaine hayyu

karka samu cikin gayyu

don ma'aikinka nabiyyu

har abokinsa aliyyu

dan uwan manzon allah

rabbana kaini garesu

lahira har in gansu

tun anan na begesu

don saboda ina sonsu

tamkacin manzon allah

yan uwa da abokanmu

ga bushara zan bamu

in nace albishirinmu

goro manya ku kawo mun

to muqara yawan sallah

muyi salati ga mahmudu

shugabana dan abdu

wanda bai aikin amdu

kuskurema ko fardu

sidi bai sabon allah

hasali akiran sallah

har iqama duk jumlah

sanda duk akace allah

sai ace manzon allah

don isarsa agun allah
