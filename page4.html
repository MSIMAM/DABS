Page Four

shugaba ibrahimu

kaulakhi ke rainon mu

shi yadau duka nauyinmu

zashi jamu shi kakkaimu

har gaban manzon allah

ya imam mune naka

namune kai ba shakka

gamu tsundum hadararka

shaikhu bamu fuyudanka

shansu ke warke illah

qoqari zan dada himma

yan uwa naga alama

ga madafa na kama

tunda naso barhama

son kwarai domin allah

nan maraya har qauye

ba abinda yaka tauye

ga magana mai qaye

yau hijabi an yaye

gamu ga manzon allah

godiya mukawa allah

mai gusarwa mutum illah

har yaketa mu'amillah

don saboda gwanin sallah

shugaba manzon allah

gamu ga dan usmanu

shaikhu ahmadu tijjani

mai sahihin imani

mai bayani da yakini

shugaba kasan allah

ga karatu ga sallah

ga yawan tsoron allah

in yaqullah ma'amillah

babu cuta ba illah

don sanin girman allah

ba qulewa ba raki

nan wajen zanja birki

don taqaitawar aiki

yan uwa ba mamaki

ba kadan baiwar allah

masu son suji wanene

ke bushara ce nine

ni aqilu mawaqine

dan aliyyu masoyine

gun mazowwa son allah

nai salati da salamu

gun ma'aikin nan namu

wanda shi ke cetonmu

kausara zai shashemu

gobe bamu ganin zillah
